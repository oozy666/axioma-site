Mailer by Axioma

Mailer by Axioma is a Telegram-based application that helps users automate email outreach by sending emails directly from their own Google accounts.

The application is designed for individual users such as music producers, beatmakers, and other creators who need a simple way to manage and send email campaigns.

Mailer by Axioma uses Google OAuth 2.0 and the Gmail API. Emails are sent strictly on behalf of the authenticated user and only after explicit user authorization.

Key Features

  • Send emails from your own Gmail account
  • No access to incoming emails
  • No reading or analysis of private messages
  • Recipient lists are processed temporarily and never stored
